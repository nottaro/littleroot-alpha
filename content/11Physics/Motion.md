## SUVAT Equations

S: Displacement: m

U: Initial Velocity: $ms^{-1}$

V: Final Velocity: $ms^{-1}$

A: Acceleration: $ms^{-2}$

T: Time: s

$$ v_{av}=\frac {s}{t} $$

$$ v=u+at $$

$$ s=ut+\frac{1}{2}at^2 $$

$$ s=vt+\frac{1}{2}at^2 $$

$$ v^2=u^2+2as $$

$$ s=(\frac {u+v}{2})t $$

## Displacement: m

$$ s=ut+\frac{1}{2}at^2 $$

$$ s=vt+\frac{1}{2}at^2 $$

$$ s=\frac{t}{2}(u+v) $$

$$ s=\frac{v^2-u^2}{2a} $$

## Initial Velocity: $ms^{-1}$

$$ u=v-at $$

$$ u=\frac{s-at^2}{2t} $$

$$ u=\frac{2s}{t}+v $$

$$ u=\sqrt{2as-v^2} $$

## Final Velocity: $ms^{-1}$

$$ v=u+at $$

$$ v=\frac{s+at^2}{2t} $$

$$ v=\frac{2s}{t}+u $$

$$ v=\sqrt{u^2+2as} $$

## Acceleration: $ms^{-2}$

$$ a=\frac{v-u}{t} $$

$$ a=\frac{2(vt-s)}{t^2} $$

$$ a=\frac{2(s-ut)}{t^2} $$

$$ a=\frac{v^2-u^2}{2s} $$

## Time: s

$$ t=\frac{v-u}{a} $$

$$ t=\frac{v- \sqrt{v^2-2as}}{a} $$

$$ t=\frac{ \sqrt{2as+u^2} -u}{a} $$

$$ t=\frac{2s}{u+v} $$
